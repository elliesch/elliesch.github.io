Hey there! I'm Ellie Abrahams.

I am an NSF Graduate Research Fellow and Two Sigma Fellow in the Departments of Astronomy and Statistics at the University of California at Berkeley.

I build physics-featurized machine learning and novel deep network architectures to study ice ocean interactions on Earth. I am also passionate about algorithmic justice and methods for the fair use of data.
